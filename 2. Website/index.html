Frequently asked questions

Do I need Spotify Premium?
Yes, Spotify Premium is required for the best experience (no ads interrupting your toddler's music). Most families already have it. We're working on Apple Music support for a future update.
What ages is this for?
Designed for toddlers aged 18 months to 5 years. The cassettes are easy for little hands to hold and tap. Kids as young as 18 months can learn the cause-and-effect of tapping to play music.
Is it safe for kids?
Absolutely. There are no batteries, no screens, and no small parts. The cassettes are made from child-safe materials and use the same technology as contactless payment cards â€” completely safe.
Does it work with any phone?
Works with any NFC-enabled smartphone (most phones made after 2018). This includes iPhone XR and newer, and most Android phones. We'll provide a compatibility check before you order.
What's included and how much does it cost?
The Starter Kit is a one-time $50 payment for 20 cassettes shipped to you. Each cassette is durable, washable, and designed to survive toddler life. The app subscription is $12/month for full access to assign and change songs. Need more cassettes? Add-on packs are $20 for 10.
Can I change which songs are on the cassettes?
Yes! You can reassign any cassette to any song on Spotify, anytime. When your child gets bored of Baby Shark (finally!), just open the app and assign a new song.
