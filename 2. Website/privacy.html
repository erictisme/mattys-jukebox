Privacy policy

Last updated: December 2025

At Mattie, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information we collect

When you use Mattie, we may collect:

  • Account information: Email address when you sign up for our waitlist or create an account
  • Spotify connection: When you connect Spotify, we access your playlists and playback controls. We do not store your Spotify password.
  • Usage data: How you use the app, which tiles are tapped, to improve our service
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, for compatibility purposes

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Mattie service
  • Send you updates about your account and our service
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send marketing communications (only if you opt in)

Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate (hosting, email, analytics)
  • Spotify: To enable music playback through their API
  • Legal requirements: If required by law

Data about children

Mattie is designed for parents to use with their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All account creation and data collection is through the parent's account.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disconnect your Spotify account at any time

Data security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email or through the app.
